🎯 HOW TO USE:

1. FILTER BY CATEGORY
   • Click "Video" to show only videos
   • Click "Audio" to show only audio files
   • Click "Document" to show documents, etc.

2. FILTER BY STATUS
   • Click "Downloading" to show active downloads
   • Click "Completed" to show finished downloads
   • Click "Failed" to show failed items

3. SEARCH
   • Type in the search box to find downloads
   • Works with or without category filter

4. CLEAR FILTERS
   • Click "🔄 Clear Filters" to show everything again

5. RIGHT-CLICK CONTEXT MENU
   • Still available on table rows
   • Pause, Resume, Cancel, Delete, etc.
